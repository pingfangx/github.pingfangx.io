[30/156] HighlightUsagesInFile

<<JumpToLastEdit  >>LayoutCode

 

Use Control+Shift+F7 (Edit | Find | Highlight Usages in File) to quickly highlight usages of some variable in the current file.

Use F3;Control+L and Shift+F3;Control+Shift+L keys to navigate through highlighted usages.

Press Escape to remove highlighting.
